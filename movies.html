Top 3 Favorite Movies

  1. The Dark Knight

    Not only is this my favorite Batman movie, this is easily one of the greatest movies I have ever watched.

    This move has intensity, drama, action, and many great themes to learn. Christian Bale and Heath Ledger play off of each other beautifully.

    The movie has my favorite villain, Joker, and Harvey Dent delivers the best line in cinema, "You either die a hero, or live long enough to see yourself become the villain"

  2. The Greatest Showman

    While P.T. Barnam was a problematic guy, Hugh Jackman does a beautiful job portraying him in this movie. This movie made me love musicals

  3. Pulp Fiction

    Pulp Fiction is a groundbreaking film that defies genre conventions with its nonlinear narrative and eclectic characters. Quentin Tarantino's direction and the ensemble cast deliver an unforgettable cinematic experience.
